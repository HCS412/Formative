Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

At Formative, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our platform and services. By using Formative, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, password, and account type (brand or influencer).

Profile Information: You may provide additional information such as business name, biography, website, and profile images.

Social Media Account Information: When you connect your social media accounts via OAuth authentication, we collect:

  • Account usernames and handles
  • Profile information (display name, bio, profile images)
  • Follower/following counts and engagement metrics
  • Content posted to connected accounts (posts, tweets, stories)
  • Account verification status

Communication Data: Messages, campaign proposals, and other communications through our platform.

Payment Information: Billing details and payment information for transactions (processed securely through third-party payment processors).

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data: Information about how you use our Service, including:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring/exit pages
  • Operating system
  • Date and time stamps

Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information and improve our Service. See Section 8 for more details.

1.3 OAuth Authentication Data

When you connect social media accounts through OAuth, we receive limited access tokens that allow us to:

  • Verify account ownership and authenticity
  • Retrieve public profile information and metrics
  • Post content on your behalf (only with explicit permission)
  • Access engagement analytics for connected accounts

We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide our services. You can revoke these permissions at any time through your account settings or the respective social media platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and Maintain the Service: To operate, maintain, and improve Formative's platform and features
  • Account Verification: To verify account ownership and prevent fraud, ensuring platform integrity
  • Facilitate Connections: To match brands with influencers and manage campaign collaborations
  • Analytics and Insights: To provide performance metrics, engagement data, and campaign analytics
  • Communication: To send service updates, respond to inquiries, and provide customer support
  • Marketing: To send promotional materials and platform updates (you can opt out at any time)
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address security issues, fraud, and abuse
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Platform Improvements: To understand usage patterns and improve user experience

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 With Other Users

Profile information and social media metrics are visible to other users to facilitate collaborations. You can control visibility settings in your account preferences.

3.2 With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who assist in:

  • Cloud hosting and data storage
  • Payment processing
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Customer support tools
  • Email and communication services

These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and may only use your information as necessary to perform their services.

3.3 Social Media Platforms

When you connect social media accounts, certain information is shared with those platforms according to their OAuth protocols and privacy policies.

3.4 Business Transfers

If Formative is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

3.5 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, subpoenas, or government agencies).

3.6 Protection of Rights

We may disclose information to:

  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Protect users or the public from harm
  • Prevent fraud or abuse

3.7 With Your Consent

We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your information within 90 days, except where we must retain it for legal or regulatory compliance.

Some information may be retained in backups for a limited period but will not be accessible for regular use.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Secure OAuth token storage and management
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Incident response procedures

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Correction

You can access and update your account information through your account settings. You may also request a copy of your personal information by contacting us.

6.2 Data Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format.

6.3 Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through account settings. Upon deletion, we will remove or anonymize your information as described in Section 4.

6.4 Social Media Connections

You can disconnect social media accounts at any time through your account settings or directly through the social media platform. This will revoke our access to that account's data.

6.5 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link or adjusting your notification preferences. Note that you will still receive transactional emails related to your account and campaigns.

6.6 Cookies

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may limit some functionality.

6.7 Do Not Track

We currently do not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals, as there is no industry-standard interpretation of DNT.

7. Regional Privacy Rights

7.1 GDPR (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Legal Basis for Processing: We process your data based on:

  • Your consent (e.g., for OAuth connections)
  • Contract performance (to provide services)
  • Legitimate interests (to improve our services, prevent fraud)
  • Legal compliance

7.2 CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to equal service and price

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@formative.com. We will verify your identity before processing requests.

7.3 Other Jurisdictions

We comply with applicable privacy laws in all jurisdictions where we operate. Contact us if you have questions about your specific rights.
